Our Heritage

Our Story

A decade of dedication to the craft, the client, and the community.

Since 2015

Born in Kathmandu,
Defined by Craft

Founded in 2015 in the vibrant streets of Thamel, Elite Fade began with a simple but powerful mission: to elevate the standard of men's grooming in Nepal. Our founder Raj Kumar noticed a gap in the market — a space between quick, low-quality haircuts and pretentious, overpriced salons.

We wanted to create a sanctuary — a place where a man could receive a world-class haircut, enjoy a luxurious hot towel shave, and relax in a masculine, refined environment without judgment or pretension.

Today, Elite Fade is more than a barber shop. It is a community hub for the modern gentleman who appreciates the finer details in life — a place where craftsmanship meets culture, and every client leaves not just looking better, but feeling better.

A Decade of Growth

Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

Raj Kumar opens Elite Fade with two barber chairs and a bold vision — to give Kathmandu the premium barber studio it deserved.

2017

Expansion

Growing demand leads to a full studio renovation — from 2 chairs to 6, with a dedicated hot towel shave station and VIP lounge.

2019

Award Winning

Voted "Best Men's Grooming Studio in Kathmandu" — recognition that validated every late night and every ounce of effort poured into this craft.

2022

Elite Fade 2.0

A full luxury renovation transforms the studio into the sleek, premium space it is today — a true destination for the modern Nepali gentleman.

2025

A Decade of Excellence

Ten years strong. Six elite barbers. Thousands of satisfied clients. And the same burning passion that started it all. The journey continues.
