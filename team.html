The Craftsmen

Meet Our Team

Six passionate barbers united by a shared love of the craft and a commitment to making every client look their absolute best.

Raj Kumar Shrestha

Classic Cuts & Fades

With over 8 years behind the chair, Raj is the heartbeat of Elite Fade. He specialises in precision fades, taper cuts, and the classic gentleman's shave. His calm, attentive approach puts every client instantly at ease.

8 Years Experience

Sanjay Tamang

Beard Artistry

Sanjay is the studio's beard maestro. From full viking sculpts to sharp corporate lines, his eye for symmetry and precision is unmatched. 5 years of dedication have made him one of Kathmandu's most sought-after beard artists.

5 Years Experience

Bikash Rai

Hair Color & Design

Bikash brings an artist's eye to hair coloring. Trained in advanced coloring techniques in India, he creates everything from natural, subtle highlights to bold, magazine-worthy looks. His clients always leave transformed.

6 Years Experience

Amir Thapa

Hot Towel Shave

Amir is the master of the hot towel shave ritual. With a steady hand and an intimate knowledge of skincare, his straight razor shaves are legendary among clients who've experienced them. Pure luxury in every stroke.

4 Years Experience

Deepak Gurung

Modern Fades

Deepak stays ahead of every trend. From high skin fades to textured crop tops, he brings contemporary barbering to Kathmandu. With 7 years of experience, he is the go-to barber for clients who want a modern edge.

7 Years Experience

Suresh Magar

Kids & Family Cuts

Suresh's gentle, fun demeanour makes him a favourite with younger clients. Patient, precise, and endlessly entertaining — he turns every child's haircut into an adventure. Families keep coming back just for him.

3 Years Experience
